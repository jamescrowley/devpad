To find some text, enter the text into the Find box, and click Find. To create a new line, press Ctrl+Enter.

 

If the textbox is not large enough, simply hold down the mouse over the bottom right hand corner and drag. To return it to its original size, right click on the corner.

 

You can retrieve previous search text by clicking the arrow next to the Find textbox, and selecting the item you want.

Enter the text you want to replace with in the Replace textbox. If it is not present, click the Replace tab first.

 

Click Replace to replace the current item, or Replace all to replace all the items in the specified search range. If no text is entered, the search text will be replaced with nothing.

The following options are available:

  • Search Range - (the Where tab). This specifies where Developers Pad will search for the text

  • Match Case - The search will be case sensitive

  • Whole Word - Only whole words will be replaced, ie if searching for he, 'he' will be replaced but 'the' will not be changed

  • Find From Cursor - The search will only take place from the cursor onwards.

  • Output Result To Log - The search results will be outputted to the Find Results window.
